How It Works

01/04

SUI Accumulation

SuiStrategy builds a growing SUI treasury through convertible debt, using stablecoin deposits to market-buy SUI, ensuring treasury growth outpaces SSTR supply for accretive dilution.

02/04

Treasury Lending

Treasury SUI is lent through a Sui-native lending pool, using SSTR as collateral, generating yield and protecting against RFV attacks with market-based defenses.

03/04

SSTR, a Leveraged Asset

SSTR offers leveraged exposure to SUI, amplifying gains with no forced liquidation, designed for transparent, DeFi- native returns.

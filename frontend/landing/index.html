How It Works

01/04

Bond-Based Structured Products

Introduce long/short bonds that fund the treasury. Deposit stablecoins to receive CDT (convertible debt tokens) + Option NFTs, which can be converted to SSTR or redeemed for underlying assets.

02/04

Smart Automation

Enable automated yield strategies via smart contract modules and note conversion mechanisms. Utilize 1inch Limit Order Protocol for TWAP execution and option-like strategies with programmable on-chain automation.

03/04

Cross-Chain Integration

Seamless cross-chain execution via 1inch Fusion+ for automated deposits and withdrawals (SUI ↔ ETH/USDC). Efficient cross-chain liquidity without the complexity of traditional bridging solutions.

04/04

Treasury Lending

Treasury SUI is lent through a Sui-native lending pool using SSTR as collateral for borrowing. Generate yield while maintaining leveraged exposure to SUI growth without liquidation risk.

CDT

Convertible Debt

0% interest debt, convertible to SSTR

Created through bonding mechanism. Protocol's market-based bonding ensures new CDT is backed by treasury assets, convertible to SSTR or redeemable for stablecoins.

NFT

Holds convertible options

Potentially more abundant than CDT. Holders must hold or acquire CDT to exercise options, sustaining consistent buy demand on the debt token for balanced tokenomics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SuiStrategy?
A DeFi protocol on Sui automating yield strategies with bonds, smart contracts, and 1inch for cross-chain swaps and composable tools. It combines structured products, automation, and cross-chain liquidity for efficient DeFi.
How do bonds work?
Long/short bonds fund the treasury. Deposit stablecoins to receive CDT (convertible debt tokens) + Option NFTs, which can be converted to SSTR for leveraged exposure or redeemed for underlying assets.
What's SSTR's value proposition?
Treasury-backed token that grows with SUI price through automated yield strategies. Value accrues from conversion mechanics, lending yields, and treasury growth without liquidation risk.
How does cross-chain work?
1inch Fusion+ handles seamless swaps (SUI ↔ ETH/USDC) for entry/exit without traditional bridging complexity. Limit Order Protocol enables TWAP and sophisticated trading strategies.
Can I borrow against SSTR?
Yes, use SSTR as collateral in the native Sui lending pool for borrowing. Maintain leveraged exposure while accessing liquidity without selling your position.
What risks are involved?
Standard DeFi risks include smart contract vulnerabilities and market volatility. However, there's no forced liquidation risk. Always DYOR and consult professionals before investing.
